About Me

I am a Backend Developer specializing in Python, Django, and FastAPI, with a proven track record of building scalable systems and outcome-driven software architecture. My focus lies in developing high-performance RESTful APIs and automating complex processes to drive operational efficiency.

My expertise includes integrating Artificial Intelligence within production-ready environments.
